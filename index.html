Ultrasonic Water Meter

LoRa Enabled Ultrasonic water meter supports standard LoRaWAN™ protocol. The ultrasonic water meter is an all-electronic water meter obtaining water flow rate by analyzing and processing water speed by detecting the time difference caused by the change of velocity when the ultrasonic beam propagates along or adverse current. It has the characteristics of low start-up flow rate, wide range ratio and high measurement accuracy. Without movable parts and flow resistant elements in the meter body, as well as impact of impurities in the water, the meter has a long service life.

Smart Energy Meter

The LoRa interface of the meters ensures a reliable and continuous connection to the gateway by constantly adjusting parameters for optimal sending and receiving. This allows the energy measurement device, based on LoRaWAN technology, to monitor and record electric energy usage in real time with unwavering dependability. Thanks to its Class C device capabilities, the energy meter can be accessed and utilized at anytime, anywhere.
